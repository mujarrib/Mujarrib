# Insomnia

Dormer and Finch's Deaths Explained.

## Body

At the end of _Insomnia_, **Dormer has lost all grip on reality** and is almost too tired to function. He also confesses the details of his aforementioned internal affairs investigation, filling the audience in on his concept of morality and his job. However, he realizes that Burr is in danger as she goes to see Finch to collect evidence, and goes after her to help her. After a chaotic hallucination on the way to Finch's boat house, Dormer arrives just in time. He and Finch fight, resulting in them both being shot. Finch dies immediately, while Dormer staggers out of the house and finds Burr.
